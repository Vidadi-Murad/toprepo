Sushi Recipe

Description

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish made with vinegared rice combined with a variety of ingredients such as raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits. It often includes nori, a type of seaweed, to wrap rolls or hold toppings. Common sushi types are maki (rolled), nigiri (rice topped with fish), and sashimi (sliced raw fish without rice). Sushi emphasizes fresh, high-quality ingredients and delicate presentation. It is usually served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
