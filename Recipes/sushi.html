Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made by layering wide pasta sheets with a mixture of meat, cheese, and sauce. Typically, ground beef or pork is cooked with onions, garlic, and tomato sauce to create a rich filling. Ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses add creaminess and flavor between the layers. The pasta sheets are usually boiled or baked directly, depending on the recipe. Once assembled, the lasagna is baked in the oven until bubbling and golden on top.
